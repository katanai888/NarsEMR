# Contributing to BansaEMR

Thank you for your interest in contributing to BansaEMR! Our mission is to make healthcare tracking accessible, intuitive, and highly localized for small clinics and standalone medical centers across the Philippines. 

By contributing, you help improve clinical workflows for community doctors, nurses, and administrative healthcare staff.

---

## 🚀 Proposing Changes

### 1. Small Fixes & Quick Tweaks
For minor bugs, documentation typos, or straightforward language updates, feel free to open a **Pull Request (PR)** directly. Please ensure your PR description clearly details what you are fixing and why.

### 2. Large Features & Structural Changes
If you want to build a major new feature, change the dashboard architecture, or introduce a new clinical module, please open a **GitHub Issue** first. Discussing your architectural approach with the maintainers beforehand helps prevent wasted effort and ensures your code aligns with our roadmap.

---
